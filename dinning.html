Case study · Dinning

Luxury hospitality with quiet confidence.

A premium digital experience that blends fine dining and overnight stay booking into one cohesive journey. The system focuses on atmosphere, deliberate hierarchy, and frictionless reservations.

Role
Lead UI/UX Designer
Type
Hospitality Product
Timeline
4 Weeks
Focus
Story-led booking flow

Overview

The challenge was to make a high-end hospitality platform feel emotionally rich without compromising clarity. The design uses clean interaction states, immersive imagery, and structured content blocks so users can discover, compare, and commit with confidence.

Design Problems Solved

Discovery friction

Users needed more visual context before selecting a dining and stay combination.

Booking complexity

Multi-step booking lacked clear pacing and confidence cues for premium purchase decisions.

Trust signals

Reviews and concierge contact needed stronger integration to reduce hesitation.

Solution Screens

The interface system is built around warm editorial contrast and restrained motion. Every major step from curation to inquiry is intentional and easy to scan.
